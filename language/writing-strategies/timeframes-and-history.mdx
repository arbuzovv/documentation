## Timeframes

The minimum backtest interval is **1 minute**. From there, you can aggregate to 2, 3, 4 minutes, or any larger interval you need — up to daily and beyond.

## History depth

Historical data is available from **2005** to the present.

| Environment | Delay |
| --- | --- |
| Russia | No delay |
| US | 15 minutes |

<Warning>
  The engine is not intended for strategies where outcomes depend on sub-second timing (high-frequency trading, latency arbitrage). See [Limitations](/language/limitations/not-for-hft).
</Warning>
