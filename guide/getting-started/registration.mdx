Registration on Ziplime is **free**. There is no cost to create an account and start describing your first strategy.

## Global Auth

Ziplime uses **Global Auth**, the shared authentication system used across Limex products. If you're already signed in to Limex, you don't need to sign in again when you open Ziplime — your session carries over automatically.

Get started at [ziplime.limex.com](http://ziplime.limex.com). See [Environment & Infrastructure](/guide/product/two-environments) for what runs underneath.

<Note>
  The free tier has limits on the number of backtest runs and strategies you can keep. Higher tiers remove these limits and unlock more capable AI models. See [Pricing & Registration](/guide/plans/pricing-and-registration).
</Note>
