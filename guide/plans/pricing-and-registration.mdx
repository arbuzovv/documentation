description: "Free tier, paid tiers, and what changes between them"
---

Registration is **free**.

Ziplime works on a **freemium** model: the free tier has limits on the number of backtest runs and the number of strategies you can keep. Tiers scale up as **Free → Pro → Max**. Higher tiers can also be granted as part of account-opening offers or marketing promotions.

## AI models by tier

The AI models available to you depend on your tier. The free tier gives access to simpler models; higher tiers (up to Max) unlock the most capable ones. In short: the higher the tier, the "smarter" the agent team working on your strategy.

<Note>
  Specific pricing hasn't been published yet — the product is still being tuned. What's stable: registration is free, and higher tiers unlock more capable AI models plus higher usage limits.
</Note>
